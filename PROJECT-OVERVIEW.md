## Word Count Management

Current approximate content: ~1,500 words (SAMPLE - needs trimming)

**Strategy for staying under 1,200 words:**
1. Count only student-written text
2. Quotes don't count toward limit
3. Navigation, titles, credits don't count
4. Edit ruthlessly - every word must earn its place
5. Use captions and blockquotes strategically

**What counts:**
- Paragraph text
- Image captions
- Timeline entries
- Biographical descriptions

**What doesn't count:**
- Quotes from sources
- Navigation menus
- Page titles/headers
- Source credits
- Bibliography
- Process paper

## Multimedia Guidelines

**Maximum 3 minutes total across entire site**

Suggested allocation:
- Audio: Thurgood Marshall oral argument (45 sec)
- Video: Brown decision news coverage (1 min 30 sec)
- Video: Little Rock Nine footage (45 sec)

**Rules:**
- Auto-playing music counts toward limit
- Can record quotes but not narrate own writing
- No externally hosted content
- All multimedia must be credited
